Immigrations to Malaysia (2020 to 2024)

In 2023, it was reported that there
are roughly 3 million immigrants
in Malaysia, making up 9.9% of
the country's population of 33.4
million. The reasons for which
people immigrate vary
across individuals, most of them
coming for work opportunities
or refuge.


Migrants in Malaysia mostly
consist of immigrants from
Asian countries, such as
Indonesia, Bangladesh, and Nepal.
These migratnts are protected by
government programmes that
support social protection schemes.
